Homework Assignment

Business Problem Solved

Apply everything you've learned to solve a real business problem. Use the RCTFC framework to craft your prompt, execute it in an AI tool, and document your results.

Task: Business Problem Solved (100 points)

Complete all 5 steps below

Step 1: Define the Problem

Write one specific business problem in 1-2 sentences. Make sure it's:

  • • Specific enough to solve
  • • Important enough to matter
  • • Within your control to address

Step 2: Craft Your RCTFC Prompt

Transform your problem into a complete RCTFC prompt:

Example Structure:

  • Role: "You are a business operations consultant..."
  • Context: "I run a [business type] with [specific challenge]..."
  • Task: "Recommend 3 actionable solutions..."
  • Format: "Present as a numbered list with pros/cons..."
  • Constraints: "Solutions must cost under $500 and take less than 2 weeks..."

Step 3: Execute the Prompt

Run your prompt in ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity. Copy or screenshot the AI's response.

ChatGPT Claude Perplexity

Step 4: Document the Response

Include the AI's complete response in your submission.

Step 5: Reflect

Answer these questions:

  • • What worked well?
  • • What would you change about your prompt?
  • • Was the solution practical for your business?

Submission Requirements

  • Submit as a Word doc, Google Doc, or PDF
  • Maximum 1 page
  • Include all 5 steps

Need Assistance?

Contact your instructor if you need help crafting your prompt or evaluating AI responses.
