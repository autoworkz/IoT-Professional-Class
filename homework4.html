Marketing & Sales Action Plan

Build on your Week 3 Gap-to-Goal Action Plan to create a complete marketing and sales strategy. Define your target customer, set pricing, and develop a 90-day action plan to close your revenue gap.

Task: Marketing & Sales Action Plan (100 points)

Complete all 5 steps below

Step 1: Review Your Gap Analysis

From your Week 3 homework, identify your key gaps:

  • • Revenue gap: How much more do you need to earn?
  • • Customer gap: How many more customers do you need?
  • • Product/service gap: What offerings need improvement?
  • • Geography gap: Where are your ideal customers?
Upload your financial documents at autoworkz.org

Step 2: Define Your Target Customer Profile

Create a detailed profile of your ideal customer:

  • Demographics: Age, income, location, industry
  • Behaviors: Where they spend time, how they buy
  • Pain Points: What problems do they have?
  • Values: What matters most to them?
  • Buying Triggers: What motivates them to purchase?

Step 3: Develop Your Marketing Strategy

Use AI with RCTFC prompts to create your marketing plan. Define:

  • • 3-5 marketing channels to reach your target customer
  • • Key messages that address their pain points
  • • Budget allocation for each channel
  • • Expected results and metrics to track
ChatGPT Claude

Step 4: Set Your Pricing Strategy

Using your Week 3 financial data, document your pricing:

  • • Cost to deliver your product/service
  • • Competitor pricing
  • • Value delivered to customer
  • • Your final price and justification

Step 5: Create Your 90-Day Action Plan

Build a timeline with specific actions:

  • • Week 1-2: Foundation activities
  • • Week 3-4: Launch first campaign
  • • Month 2: Scale what works
  • • Month 3: Evaluate and adjust
Success Metrics: Define how you'll measure success (leads, conversions, revenue, customer acquisition cost).

Submission Requirements

  • Submit as a Word doc, Google Doc, or PDF
  • Maximum 2 pages
  • Include all 5 steps
