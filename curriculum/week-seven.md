## Lesson Content

### What is the Capstone Project?
The capstone is your chance to demonstrate everything you've learned by creating a real AI-powered solution to a real problem.

### Project Requirements
Your project should demonstrate:
- **Problem Definition**: Clear, specific problem statement
- **AI Application**: Use of RCTFC, forecasting, or automation
- **Implementation**: Working solution (or detailed plan)
- **Documentation**: Clear process description

### Choosing Your Project
Consider projects that:
- Solve a real problem you face
- Use tools you've learned (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, OpenCode)
- Can be completed in 1-2 weeks
- Provide measurable value

### Project Ideas
- Business problem solving with AI
- Marketing forecast for your business
- Automation of a repetitive task
- Data analysis and insights
- Customer communication system

## In-Class Activities

### Activity 1: Project Scope Definition (20 minutes)
**Objective**: Define your project

**Steps**:
1. Define your capstone project:
   - **Problem**: What are you solving?
   - **Approach**: Which AI tools/frameworks will you use?
   - **Outcome**: What does success look like?
2. Write a one-paragraph project summary
3. Get instructor approval

---

### Activity 2: Design Sprint - Plan Your Solution (25 minutes)
**Objective**: Create your project plan

**Steps**:
1. Create a project plan with:
   - Problem statement
   - RCTFC prompts needed
   - Data requirements (if any)
   - Automation opportunities (if any)
   - Timeline and milestones
2. Sketch the solution architecture

---

### Activity 3: Build Session - Hands-On Development (35 minutes)
**Objective**: Start building your solution

**Steps**:
1. Begin building your solution:
   - Test prompts in ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity
   - Use OpenCode for any coding needs
   - Document your process
2. Instructor available for guidance

---

### Activity 4: Peer Feedback & Iteration (10 minutes)
**Objective**: Improve through feedback

**Steps**:
1. Share your project plan with a partner
2. Receive feedback on clarity and feasibility
3. Adjust scope if needed
4. Prepare for final presentation

## Homework Assignment

### Due: Before Week 8

**Total Points**: 100

---

### Task: Final Project Completion (100 points)

**Requirements**:
1. **Complete** your AI-powered solution
2. **Document** your process:
   - Problem definition
   - Tools and frameworks used
   - Prompts crafted
   - Results achieved
3. **Prepare** a 5-minute presentation:
   - The problem you solved
   - Your approach and tools
   - The solution and results
   - What you learned

**Submission Requirements**:
- Format: Word doc, Google Doc, or PDF
- Length: 2-3 pages
- Include presentation slides or notes
