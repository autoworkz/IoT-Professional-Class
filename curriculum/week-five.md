## In-Class Activities

### Activity 1: Data Preparation Workshop (40 minutes)
**Objective**: Inventory and prepare your business data for AI analysis

**Steps**:
1. Inventory your existing business data sources:
   - Sales records and revenue data
   - Customer lists and contact information
   - Marketing campaign results
   - Website and social media analytics
2. Export and format your data for AI analysis:
   - Remove duplicates and errors
   - Ensure consistent date formats (MM/DD/YYYY)
   - Label columns clearly
   - Create a summary sheet with key metrics
   - Save as .xlsx file for upload

---

### Activity 2: AI Forecasting Lab (50 minutes)
**Objective**: Generate AI-powered forecasts using the RCTFC framework

**Prompt Structure**:
- **Role**: "You are a marketing analytics expert specializing in small business forecasting."
- **Context**: "This is data from [your business type] covering [time period]."
- **Task**: "Analyze trends and forecast [leads/conversions/revenue] for the next 90 days."
- **Format**: "Provide a trend summary, 90-day forecast with numbers, and confidence level."
- **Constraints**: "Focus on [specific product/service/segment]."

**Steps**:
1. Upload your Excel file to ChatGPT or Claude
2. Enter your customized RCTFC prompt
3. Document the AI's analysis
4. Identify 3 actionable marketing decisions based on the forecast

## Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Forecasting** | Predicting future trends based on historical data |
| **Trend** | Direction of change over time |
| **Confidence Level** | AI's estimate of how reliable a prediction is |
