## Lesson Content

### Why Forecast?
Forecasting helps you:
- Allocate resources effectively
- Plan marketing campaigns
- Reduce uncertainty
- Make data-driven decisions

### Types of Business Data
| Data Type | Examples | Forecasting Use |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| Sales Data | Revenue, units sold, transactions | Predict future sales |
| Customer Data | Acquisition, retention, lifetime value | Predict customer behavior |
| Marketing Data | Campaign results, ad performance | Optimize marketing spend |
| Web Analytics | Traffic, conversions, engagement | Predict trends |

### Preparing Data for AI
Before using AI with your data:
1. **Audit**: What data do you have?
2. **Clean**: Remove duplicates, fix errors
3. **Format**: Consistent date formats, clear labels
4. **Summarize**: Create summary metrics

---

## In-Class Activities

### Activity 1: Data Audit - What Data Do You Have? (15 minutes)
**Objective**: Inventory your data assets

**Steps**:
1. List all business data sources:
   - Sales records
   - Customer lists
   - Marketing campaign results
   - Website/social media analytics
2. Identify which data is most relevant for forecasting

---

### Activity 2: Export & Prep - Formatting Excel for AI (20 minutes)
**Objective**: Prepare data for AI analysis

**Steps**:
1. Export data from your systems (CRM, POS, spreadsheets)
2. Clean and format for AI analysis:
   - Remove duplicates and errors
   - Ensure consistent date formats
   - Label columns clearly
   - Create a summary sheet with key metrics
3. Review best practices for data structure

---

### Activity 3: AI Forecasting Lab - Upload, Analyze, Predict (35 minutes)
**Objective**: Generate AI-powered forecasts

**Prompt Structure**:
- **Role**: "You are a marketing analytics expert..."
- **Context**: "This is [business type] data from [time period]..."
- **Task**: "Analyze trends and forecast [metric] for the next 3 months..."
- **Format**: "Provide summary of trends, 3-month forecast, confidence level..."
- **Constraints**: "Focus on [specific product/service/segment]..."

**Steps**:
1. Upload your Excel file to ChatGPT or Claude
2. Enter your forecasting prompt
3. Document the AI's analysis

---

### Activity 4: Strategy Translation - From Forecast to Action (20 minutes)
**Objective**: Turn insights into action

**Steps**:
1. Review AI-generated insights
2. Identify 3 actionable marketing decisions:
   - What to **increase**
   - What to **decrease**
   - What to **test**
3. Share one insight with the class

## Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Forecasting** | Predicting future trends based on historical data |
| **Trend** | Direction of change over time |
| **Confidence Level | An estimate of how reliable a prediction is |
