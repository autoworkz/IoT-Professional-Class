## Core Concepts

### The RCTFC Framework
Every effective prompt has 5 components:

| Component | Definition | Example |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| **R**ole | Persona or expertise to adopt | "You are a conversion copywriter" |
| **C**ontext | Background information | "I run a small bakery targeting busy professionals" |
| **T**ask | Specific action to perform | "Write a promotional email for our new lunch delivery service" |
| **F**ormat | How output should be structured | "Use a friendly tone, keep it under 150 words, include a CTA" |
| **C**onstraints | Limitations to apply | "Don't mention competitors or use the word 'delicious'" |

### Iteration is Key
- Great prompts rarely happen on the first try
- Refine, adjust, and build on AI responses

### Context is Queen
- More relevant context = better results
- Your OCEAN profile is context
- Your business goals are context
- Your constraints are context

### Different AIs, Different Strengths

| AI Tool | Best For |
|---------|----------|
| **ChatGPT** | Creative writing, brainstorming, general tasks |
| **Claude** | Analysis, nuanced reasoning, longer context |
| **Perplexity** | Research, current events, cited sources |

## In-Class Activities

### Activity 1: RCTFC Framework Demo (20 min)
- Watch instructor demonstrate transforming a vague prompt into a powerful one
- **Example Transformation**:
  - Before: "Write me a marketing email"
  - After: "You are a conversion copywriter (Role). I run a small bakery targeting busy professionals (Context). Write a promotional email for our new lunch delivery service (Task). Use a friendly tone, keep it under 150 words, include a call-to-action button text (Format). Don't mention competitors or use the word 'delicious' (Constraints)."

### Activity 2: Prompt Engineering Lab (30 min)
- Using your problem from Senseii Games (Week 2), craft a RCTFC prompt
- Test it on all three platforms:
  - ChatGPT
  - Claude
  - Perplexity

### Activity 3: Compare & Refine (25 min)
- Analyze the three responses
- Which AI gave the best answer for your specific need?
- Refine your prompt and run it again

**Pro Tip**: Your OCEAN profile affects how you communicate:
- High Openness? You might prefer creative prompts
- High Conscientiousness? You'll want structured outputs

### Activity 4: Build Your Prompt Library (15 min)
Start a personal "Prompt Library" document with template starters:

```
Business: "You are a [role] helping a [business type]..."
Personal: "Act as my personal [coach/advisor/assistant]..."
Research: "Find and summarize [topic] with sources..."
```

## Homework Assignment

### Part 1: Strategic AI Thinking (Choose ONE question, 3-5 sentences)
- **Question A**: Think about a decision you made recently. How might AI have helped you make it faster, with more confidence, or with better data?
- **Question B**: What's one area where you spend too much time on repetitive tasks? How could automation or AI reduce that burden?

### Part 2: Hands-On Practice (Complete BOTH tasks)
- **Task 1**: Go to ChatGPT, Copilot, or Gemini and enter: *"Help me write a 3-step checklist to improve customer follow-up in my business."* Copy/screenshot the result.
- **Task 2**: Think about your home or workplace. Name one automated system you interact with regularly (e.g., smart thermostat, auto-reply email). Answer: How does this system save you time or improve accuracy?

**Submission Requirements**: Word doc, Google Doc, or PDF (1 page max)
