# Week 2: Senseii Games Challenge

## Theme
Problem-Solving & Resilience through Gamification

## Tagline
*"Mastering Problem Solving through Gamification"*

## Strategic Takeaway
> *"The quality of your output is determined by the quality of your input. Treat AI as a partner, not a search engine."*

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of Week 2, learners will be able to:
1. Understand the importance of prompt precision
2. Learn to iterate and refine your approach based on feedback
3. Apply structured thinking to solve complex problems
4. Build resilience through challenging gameplay

---

## In-Class Activities

### Activity 1: Access Senseii Games (5 min)
- Go to senseiigames.com
- Create an account or log in
- Familiarize yourself with the interface

### Activity 2: Complete Maze Levels 1-5 (30 min)
- Work through each maze level sequentially
- Pay attention to patterns and dead ends
- Build problem-solving skills and resilience
- Note your strategies for each level

### Activity 3: Track Your Progress (10 min)
- Record your completion time for each level
- Note the number of attempts per level
- Identify which strategies worked best
- Prepare for homework challenges (Levels 1-9)

---

## Homework Assignment

### Task 1: Maze Levels 1-9
Complete the advanced maze challenges to test your navigation skills.

### Task 2: Plane Levels 1-5
Master the flight simulation levels.
