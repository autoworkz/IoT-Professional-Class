# Week 8: Final Presentations

## Theme
Showcase Your Mastery

## Tagline
*"Showcase Your Mastery: From Learner to AI Whisperer"*

## Strategic Takeaway
> *"Teaching others what you've learned is the ultimate proof of mastery."*

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of Week 8, learners will be able to:
1. Present their capstone project to an audience
2. Articulate their AI problem-solving approach
3. Receive and incorporate feedback
4. Preview advanced AI concepts (Agent Swarms)

---

## In-Class Activities

### Activity 1: Final Presentations (60 min)
- Each student presents for 5 minutes
- Presentation format:
  - Problem you solved
  - Tools and approach used
  - Solution and results
  - Lessons learned
- 2-minute Q&A after each presentation

### Activity 2: Peer Feedback (15 min)
- Provide constructive feedback to classmates
- Vote on:
  - Most innovative solution
  - Best presentation
  - Most practical application

### Activity 3: Course Reflection (10 min)
- What did you learn about yourself?
- How will you use AI differently going forward?
- What would you like to learn next?
- Complete the course reflection form

### Activity 4: Bonus Preview – Agent Swarms (5 min)
- Introduction to multi-agent AI systems
- How AI agents can work together
- Future applications and possibilities
- Preview of advanced AI concepts
