# Week 1: Data, AI & You

## Theme
Personal Data Exploration for Business Intelligence

## Tagline
*"Data as the New Gold & AI as the Data Specialist"*

## Strategic Takeaway
> *"Small businesses that win are not the ones with the most technology, but the ones who ask better questions of their data."*

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of Week 1, learners will be able to:
1. Recognize behavioral data as a core input to business intelligence
2. Understand how AI interprets human data patterns
3. See practical applications in leadership & operations
4. Build confidence with AI-powered decision tools

---

## In-Class Activities

### Activity 1: Access SenseiiWyze (10 min)
- Log in to the SenseiiWyze platform
- Navigate to the assessment section
- Prepare for your data exploration journey

### Activity 2: Complete Big 5 (OCEAN) Assessment (20 min)
Take the personality assessment to generate your behavioral data profile.

**OCEAN Framework:**
- **O**penness: Creativity, curiosity, willingness to try new things
- **C**onscientiousness: Organization, reliability, follow-through
- **E**xtraversion: Social energy, assertiveness, communication
- **A**greeableness: Cooperation, trust, helpfulness
- **N**euroticism: Emotional sensitivity, stress response

### Activity 3: Review Results Privately (15 min)
- Analyze your results within the app
- Identify your dominant traits
- Consider how these traits affect your business decisions
- Note any surprises or insights
