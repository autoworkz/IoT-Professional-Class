## Lesson Content

### Presentation Guidelines
Your presentation should be 5 minutes covering:

1. **The Problem** (1 min)
   - What challenge did you address?
   - Why does it matter?

2. **Your Approach** (1 min)
   - Which AI tools did you use?
   - What framework guided your work?

3. **The Solution** (2 min)
   - What did you build/create?
   - How does it work?

4. **Lessons Learned** (1 min)
   - What surprised you?
   - What would you do differently?

### Handling Questions
- Listen fully to the question
- Answer honestly
- Admit if you don't know something
- It's okay to say "That's a great question, I hadn't considered that"

### Giving Feedback
Constructive feedback includes:
- What worked well
- Specific suggestions for improvement
- Questions for clarification
