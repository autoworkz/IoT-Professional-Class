Business Vision: Key Questions to Answer

Define your business future with these 6 essential elements:

1. Revenue Goals

What is your target annual revenue for the next 3-5 years?

2. Customer Type

Who is your ideal customer? What makes them the perfect fit?

3. New Product/Expansion

What new products or services will you offer?

4. New Geography

What new markets or locations will you serve?

5. Salary for Employees/Self

What compensation goals do you have for yourself and your team?

6. Hire New Employee

What roles will you need to fill to achieve your vision?
