8 Key Elements for Your Vision Board

Include these elements to create a complete picture of your business vision:

1. Personal Identity

Who you are as a leader - strengths, triggers, how you show up under pressure

2. Core Values

Foundational values: sustainability, innovation, integrity

3. Vision Alignment

How personal goals align with business direction

4. Customer Persona

Who you're serving - demographics, pain points, needs

5. Brand Identity

Look and feel - branding, website, workspace aesthetics

6. Dream Team

People needed - hiring, outsourcing, team culture

7. Leadership Skills

Emotional intelligence, conflict resolution, team dynamics

8. Metrics and Growth

KPIs and milestones - not just abstract dreams
