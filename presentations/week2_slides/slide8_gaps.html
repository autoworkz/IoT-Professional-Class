Finding and Fixing the Gaps

With your 3 data points, you can now see the gaps between where you are and where you want to be:

Leadership Gaps

If your OCEAN profile shows low emotional resilience but your vision requires building a team - there is a leadership development gap.

Tech Skill Gaps

If your vision includes AI-powered operations but your tech readiness score is low - there is a skill development gap.

Identity Gaps

If your core values do not align with your business direction - there is an identity conflict gap causing internal stress.

Resource Gaps

If your vision requires a dream team but you lack leadership skills to manage people - there is a resource gap.

Your 12-Month Focus: Create a development plan that closes the gaps between your current reality and your business vision.
