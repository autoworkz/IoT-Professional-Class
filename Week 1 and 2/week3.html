This Week's Mission

Transform from AI user to AI whisperer. You'll learn the frameworks and techniques that turn vague requests into precise, powerful prompts that deliver consistent results across ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.

Core Concepts

The Anatomy of a Perfect Prompt

Every effective prompt has 5 components: Role, Context, Task, Format, and Constraints. We call this the RCTFC Framework.

Iteration is Key

Great prompts rarely happen on the first try. Learn to refine, adjust, and build on AI responses to get closer to your goal with each exchange.

Context is Queen

The more relevant context you provide, the better your results. Your OCEAN profile from Week 1 is context. Your business goals are context. Your constraints are context.

Different AIs, Different Strengths

ChatGPT: Creative writing, brainstorming, general tasks. Claude: Analysis, nuanced reasoning, longer context. Perplexity: Research, current events, cited sources.

Step 1 ~20 min

The RCTFC Framework Demo

Watch instructor demonstrate transforming a vague prompt into a powerful one using Role, Context, Task, Format, and Constraints. Take notes on the before/after.

Example Transformation:

❌ "Write me a marketing email"

✅ "You are a conversion copywriter (Role). I run a small bakery targeting busy professionals (Context). Write a promotional email for our new lunch delivery service (Task). Use a friendly tone, keep it under 150 words, include a call-to-action button text (Format). Don't mention competitors or use the word 'delicious' (Constraints)."

Analyze the three responses. Which AI gave the best answer for your specific need? Refine your prompt based on what you learned and run it again. Share your best result with the class.

💡 Pro Tip: Your OCEAN profile affects how you communicate. High Openness? You might prefer creative prompts. High Conscientiousness? You'll want structured outputs.

Step 4 ~15 min

Build Your Prompt Library

Start a personal "Prompt Library" document. Save your best prompts with notes on what worked. This becomes your AI toolkit for life and business.

Template Starters:

  • • Business: "You are a [role] helping a [business type]..."
  • • Personal: "Act as my personal [coach/advisor/assistant]..."
  • • Research: "Find and summarize [topic] with sources..."
