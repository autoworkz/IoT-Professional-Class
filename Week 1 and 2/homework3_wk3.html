Prompt Engineering Practice

Week 3 Homework Assignment

Estimated Time

1.5 - 2 hours

Due Date

Before Week 4

Total Points

100 Points

Assignment Overview

This homework reinforces your RCTFC prompt engineering skills through real-world practice. You'll create prompts for business and personal scenarios, compare AI responses, and begin building your permanent prompt library.

1

Business Prompt Challenge

25 Points

Create a complete RCTFC prompt for a business scenario relevant to your work or entrepreneurial goals.

Requirements:

  • Clearly label each RCTFC component (Role, Context, Task, Format, Constraints)
  • Test on at least 2 different AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity)
  • Include screenshots of both AI responses
  • Write a 2-3 sentence comparison of which AI performed better and why

💡 Scenario Ideas: Marketing copy, customer email response, product description, meeting agenda, project proposal, social media content

2

Personal Life Prompt Challenge

25 Points

Create a complete RCTFC prompt for a personal life scenario—something that helps you outside of work.

Requirements:

  • Clearly label each RCTFC component
  • Incorporate your OCEAN personality insights from Week 1 into the prompt context
  • Test on your preferred AI platform
  • Include screenshot and explain how your OCEAN profile influenced the prompt

💡 Scenario Ideas: Meal planning, fitness advice, learning roadmap, home organization, travel planning, habit building, relationship communication

3

Prompt Iteration Exercise

25 Points

Take one of your prompts from Task 1 or 2 and iterate on it at least 3 times to improve the result.

Document Your Journey:

  • Version 1: Your original prompt + AI response
  • Version 2: First refinement + what you changed and why
  • Version 3: Second refinement + what you changed and why
  • Final Version: Your best prompt + final AI response

🎯 Goal: Show how small changes in prompts lead to significantly different (and better) outputs. This is the core skill of prompt engineering!

4

Start Your Prompt Library

25 Points

Create a personal prompt library document with at least 5 reusable prompts organized by category.

Library Requirements:

  • Organize prompts by category (Business, Personal, Research, Creative, etc.)
  • For each prompt, include: Name, Category, The Prompt, Best AI to Use, Notes
  • At least 2 prompts for business use
  • At least 2 prompts for personal use
  • At least 1 prompt for research/learning

📚 Example Library Entry:

Name: Email Response Generator

Category: Business Communication

Prompt: "You are a professional business communicator. I received this email: [paste email]. Write a polite, professional response that [goal]. Keep it under 150 words and maintain a [tone] tone."

Best AI: Claude (nuanced, professional tone)

Notes: Works great for customer complaints

Submission

Submit the following:

  • A single document (PDF, Word, or Google Doc) containing all 4 tasks
  • Screenshots embedded in the document (or attached separately)
  • Your Prompt Library (can be in the same doc or a separate file)

Drag and drop your files here, or

Accepted: PDF, DOC, DOCX, PNG, JPG (Max 25MB total)
