# IoT Professional Class - AI Whisperer Course

## Project Overview

The **IoT Professional Class** (also branded as the **AI Whisperer Course**) is an 8-week professional development program created by **Automation Workz**. This course teaches learners to leverage AI tools for business intelligence, data-driven decision making, and personal productivity.

---

## Course Philosophy

> *"Small businesses that win are not the ones with the most technology, but the ones who ask better questions of their data."*

The course emphasizes:
- **Data as strategic asset**: Understanding behavioral, operational, and personality data
- **AI as partner, not search engine**: Treating AI as a collaborative tool for decision-making
- **Practical application**: Hands-on exercises with real AI tools
- **Personalized learning**: Connecting content to individual personality profiles (OCEAN)

---

## Mission Statement

Help people identify a problem, procedurally solve it with AI, and apply it to life and business—equipping them with the right tools to achieve their goals.

---

## Course Goal

**Primary Goal**: Transform small business owners and professionals into "AI Whisperers" who leverage AI as a strategic partner for data-driven decision-making.

---

## Feature-Benefit-Goal Bridge

| Feature | Benefit | Goal Alignment |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| **OCEAN Assessment** | Self-awareness of behavioral tendencies; align people with roles/teams | Make data personal—see yourself as data |
| **Senseii Games** | Resilience, adaptability, strategic thinking | Build problem-solving mindset for AI iteration |
| **RCTFC Framework** | Produce quality AI outputs consistently | Transform AI from search engine → strategic partner |
| **Multi-AI Testing** | Know which tool for which job | Develop AI literacy and tool fluency |
| **Prompt Library** | Reusable assets for efficiency | Operationalize AI in daily work |
| **Weekly Quizzes** | Reinforce key concepts | Validate retention of principles |
| **Homework Assignments** | Hands-on application | Bridge theory → practice |
| **Vision Boards** | Clarify personal/business direction | Connect AI tools to real goals |

---

## Core Principles

These principles guide the design of quizzes, homework, and participation activities:

1. **Practical over theoretical** — Every task must connect to real business/personal use
2. **Iteration is essential** — Reward refinement, not just completion
3. **Personalization matters** — Use OCEAN insights to tailor the experience
4. **AI as partner, not oracle** — Students must *direct* AI, not just receive answers
5. **Data-driven reflection** — Every output should prompt "what does this tell me?"
