AI for Marketing Forecasting

Use AI to analyze your business data and generate a 90-day marketing forecast. You will use the RCTFC prompt structure learned in class.

Task: Marketing Forecast Report (100 points)

Complete all 4 tasks below

Task 1: Prepare Your Data (20 points)

Export and clean your own Excel data (sales, marketing, or customer data)

  • Remove duplicates and errors
  • Ensure consistent date formats (MM/DD/YYYY)
  • Label columns clearly
  • Create a summary sheet with key metrics

Take a screenshot of your formatted spreadsheet

Task 2: Run the AI Forecast Prompt (30 points)

Copy and customize this prompt in ChatGPT or Claude:

--- COPY THIS PROMPT ---

Role: You are a marketing analytics expert specializing in small business forecasting.

Context: I run a [describe your business type and industry]. I have attached data showing [describe your data] from [start date] to [end date]. My business goal is [state your goal].

Task: Analyze my data and help me understand:

  1. What trends do you see?
  2. What patterns should I pay attention to?
  3. What is your 90-day forecast?
  4. What 3 marketing actions should I take?

Format: Trend Summary, Key Patterns, 90-Day Forecast, Recommended Actions, Risk Factors

Constraints: Focus on practical insights. Avoid technical jargon.

--- END PROMPT ---

Screenshot or copy the complete AI response

ChatGPT Claude

Task 3: Action Plan (30 points)

Based on the AI forecast, list 3 specific marketing actions:

  • What you will increase
  • What you will decrease
  • What you will test

Include target dates for each action

Task 4: Reflection (20 points)

Answer in 1-2 paragraphs:

  • What surprised you most about your data trends?
  • How will this change your marketing decisions?
  • What additional data would help make better forecasts?

Submission Requirements

  • Submit as Word doc, Google Doc, or PDF
  • Maximum 2-3 pages
  • Include: Data screenshot, AI response, action plan, reflection
